sCalendar Support

Thank you for using sCalendar! We're here to help you get the most out of your calendar experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new event?

Tap the "+" button in the top navigation bar, or tap on any day in the calendar to create a new event for that date.

How do I switch between views?

Tap the calendar icon in the top navigation bar to cycle through Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and Yearly views.

How do I set a default calendar?

Go to Settings (menu icon) and select your preferred default calendar under "Default Calendar".

How do I change the first day of the week?

Go to Settings and choose between Monday or Sunday under "Week Starts On".

How do I create recurring events?

When creating or editing an event, use the "Repeat" option to set daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly recurrence.

How do I set event reminders?

When creating or editing an event, use the "Alert" option to set a reminder from 1 week before to the time of the event.

Why can't I see my calendars?

Make sure you've granted calendar access to sCalendar. Go to iOS Settings → sCalendar → Calendars and enable access.
